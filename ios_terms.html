02

Description of the App

SnapSize is an image processing tool for iOS that enables users to compress, resize, crop, and convert photos (JPEG, PNG, HEIC/HEIF) entirely on-device.

Key characteristics:

  • Fully offline — all processing happens on your device; no internet connection required for core features
  • No account required — the App can be used anonymously without registration
  • No data collection — your photos are never transmitted to Durusoft's servers
  • SnapSize Widget — an optional home screen widget showing your savings statistics, sharing data only within the App Group group.com.durusoft.snapsize

Free Tier & Usage Limits

SnapSize is available free of charge with the following usage limits:

3/day

Compressions per day, resetting at midnight local time. Up to 5 photos per batch.

Pro — Unlimited

100/batch

No daily cap. Process up to 100 photos per batch. No ads.

Offline (Free)

1/batch

When offline, free users can process 1 photo at a time.
