Offline Usage Policy

SnapSize includes an offline access management system designed to maintain fair usage and support the app's continued development. This system applies only to free-tier users; Pro users have unrestricted offline access.

Free tier offline rules:

  • Online: Batch processing is available for up to 10 photos per batch with no restrictions.
