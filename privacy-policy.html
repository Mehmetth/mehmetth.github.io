Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the Status Saver - Save Download app (“we,” “our,” or “the app”) handles user information and protects your privacy. We understand the importance of your personal data and are fully committed to maintaining transparency, integrity, and security.

We do not collect or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) from users of the app. All features of the app—including viewing, downloading, or managing WhatsApp status files—are handled locally on your device.

3. Third-Party Services

The app integrates Google AdMob to serve advertisements. While we do not share any data ourselves, AdMob may collect information (e.g., device information, ad interactions) to personalize ads in accordance with their own privacy policy. We do not have control over their data handling mechanisms.

You can learn more about AdMob’s data practices here: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

4. Permissions

To deliver its core functionality, the app may request the following permissions:

  • Storage Access – To read and save media files from your WhatsApp statuses.
  • Network Access – To serve advertisements and check connectivity.
  • Notification Access – To alert users about saved statuses (optional).

These permissions are used solely to provide a seamless user experience and are not abused or accessed beyond their intended purpose.

5. Children’s Privacy

This app is not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware of any such data being inadvertently collected, we will delete it immediately.

6. Data Security

While no app can guarantee complete security, we make every effort to ensure that your usage remains local, anonymous, and private. No external database or cloud is used to store user data.
